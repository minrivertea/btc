Make money by exploiting BTC arbitrage opportunities

We offer data and analysis on Bitcoin (BTC) prices between major exchanges to help you identify and (hopefully) profit from arbitrage opportunities.

What is arbitrage?
Arbitrage is where you make a profit by taking advantages of price differences in different markets. Buy in NY for a dollar, sell in Shanghai for two.

How can I use this website?
You can use this site to analyse and identify arbitrage opportunities in the BTC market. We don't facilitate trades anywhere on the site; we're not an exchange.
