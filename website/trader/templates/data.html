Some notes on our data

This information isn't essential, but it will help you understand how we make our calculations, to what degree they apply to you and with what degree of accuracy.

Trade Fees
We manually track trade fees on the various exchanges. In all cases, we apply the highest fee, and don't take into account any VIP or high-volume discounts. For example, Mt Gox. charges 0.5% fee for buy/sell trades for low-volume, non-VIP users - we use this amount to calculate MtGox trading fees.

FX Data
Our 'idealised' FX rates are sourced from www.openexchangerates.com. These are updated daily at present. 'Real-world' currently only applies to Chinese Yuan (RMB) - data is sourced from Bank of China, which generally offers the best buy/sell rates on the market.

API Trade Data
Trade data comes direct from exchange APIs, which are open and easily accessible. We look for last-buy price, and if not available, the stated 'buy' price, or an average of the last X trades provided.

Our Bitcoin price data is currently refreshed every {{ scrape_interval }} minutes. We're working to make this faster.
